~Message of the Day~

~Attention energizes; intention transforms~

Today we look at how we can live carefree - free from judgment and anxiety, focusing on the joy and perfection of what is right now.

By meditating and plunging into the awareness of the present moment, you understand that it is at this moment, everything is perfect and is what it should be and that any problems that you have are transient and temporary.

Throughout life, you will begin to look at everyday circumstances easily and meet surprises calmly and with grace.

You reconnect with your True Self, feeling lighter and free.

QUESTION No 1: How do you feel when you wake up every morning?

QUESTION No 2: How much would your life improve if you lived with a light and carefree heart?

QUESTION No 3: How can you switch your mind to spread the love and joy that you feel in your heart right now?
